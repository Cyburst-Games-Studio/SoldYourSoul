This is a reconstruction of my college capstone project made in Unity 6 using what I learned to improve upon previous design.

Game Summary
  Venture into a version of the underworld to reclaim your soul after you sell it to the devil.
  The central mechanic combines the player's health and currency into one stat, forcing the player to sacrifice their survivability to purchase upgrades.

Levels
  The game will consist of 4-5 levels, starting with the Caves and ending on the Castle.
  Levels between these two will be chosen at random based on a set list.
  Each level is unique in its terrain, enemies, and obstacles.
  All levels will feature an optional mini boss to bonus health and rewards and required boss to progress.
